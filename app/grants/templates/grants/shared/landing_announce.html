"The Mathematically Optimal Way to Democratically Fund Public Goods" - Glen Weyl


Quadratic Funding measures the number of contributors to your grants rather than the size of their contributions.
This means that grants that are widely supported receive WAY more matching funds. Sometimes, this means a $1 contribution can create $300+ in matching (but only if the grant is widely democratically supported).
